Project: Next OTrain

Ottawa, Ontario is home to the OTrain, a lightrail train that travels between 5 stops, the middle of which is my school, Carleton University. As a daily OTrain commuter, I created a webapp for telling me when the next one would arrive at each station.

...and if a screenshot isn't enough, you can see it live at:
